Authentication is done through firebase. 

After agreeing to terms and conditions you will be navigated to the recipes list. 

It is a recipe app in which you can add recipes as your favorite recipes and you can also delete favorite recipes from favorite recipes list. 

There is a drawer in the top left of the page through which you can navigate to other pages.

In the top right there is a setting icon on tapping it you will be navigated to the setting page from where you can navigate to other pages include change password, about page, contact etc. 

You can also change your password. After changing password you will be required to login again.
